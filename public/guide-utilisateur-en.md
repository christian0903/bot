# User Guide - Back on Track

## Getting Started

### Sign Up
1. Click **Login** in the header
2. Select the **Sign Up** tab
3. Fill in the form: display name, email, password
4. Answer the verification question
5. Click **Sign Up**
6. Check your email and click the confirmation link

### Login
1. Click **Login**
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Sign In**

### Forgot Password
1. On the login page, click **Forgot password?**
2. Enter your email
3. Click **Send Reset Link**
4. Check your email and follow the link to create a new password

## Navigation

- **Home**: Studio announcements and quick access
- **Schedule**: View all available classes and book
- **My Bookings**: History of your bookings (upcoming and past)
- **My Packs**: Your purchased packs and remaining credits
- **Buy a Pack**: Catalog of available packs

## Booking a Class

1. Go to **Schedule**
2. Navigate with arrows to change weeks
3. Toggle between week view and list view with the buttons at the top right
4. Click **Book** on the desired class
5. If you have available credits of the right type, the booking is confirmed
6. If you don't have credits, you'll be prompted to buy a pack

## Buying a Pack

1. Go to **Buy a Pack**
2. Browse packs available for your category
3. Click **Buy** on your chosen pack
4. You'll be redirected to the secure Stripe payment page
5. After payment, your credits are automatically activated

## Profile

1. Click your avatar in the header → **Profile**
2. Edit your name, first name, phone, bio, and member category
3. Click **Save**

## Themes and Language

- **Language**: Click **FR** or **EN** in the header to switch language
- **Theme**: Click the sun/moon icon in the header to choose from 4 themes:
  - Classic (light)
  - Dark
  - Vivid (colorful)
  - Vivid Dark

## Notifications

- The bell in the header shows the number of unread notifications
- Click it to see all your notifications
- You can mark all notifications as read

## Mobile App (PWA)

Back on Track is a Progressive Web App. To install it:
- **iPhone/iPad**: Open Safari → Share button → "Add to Home Screen"
- **Android**: Chrome → Menu (⋮) → "Install app"
- **Desktop**: Chrome → Address bar → Install icon
