# User Guide — Back on Track

## Welcome

Back on Track is your fitness studio's app. It lets you view the class schedule, book sessions, manage your credit packs, and track your activity.

---

## Create an Account

1. Click **Login** in the header
2. Select the **Sign Up** tab
3. Fill in the form:
   - **Display name**: your first name or nickname (letters, spaces, and hyphens only)
   - **Email**: your email address
   - **Password**: minimum 8 characters
   - **Confirm password**: retype your password
   - **Verification question**: answer the simple math question
4. Click **Sign Up**
5. **Check your email**: you'll receive a confirmation link. Your account will only be active after clicking it.

> **Note**: if you can't find the email, check your spam folder.

## Log In

1. Click **Login**
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Sign In**
4. You'll be redirected to your dashboard

## Forgot Password

1. On the login page, click the **Forgot password?** tab
2. Enter your email address
3. Click **Send Reset Link**
4. Check your email and click the link
5. Choose a new password (minimum 8 characters)

## Dashboard

After logging in, your dashboard shows:

- **Personalized welcome** message
- **Quick access**: two clickable cards to the Schedule and pack purchase
- **My credits**: your active packs with a colored progress bar for each
  - Color indicates credit type (blue = semi-private, orange = personal training)
  - Days remaining until expiry are shown (in red if < 14 days)
- **Upcoming classes**: your next bookings with date, time, and coach

## Class Schedule

### Access the Schedule
Click **Schedule** in the navigation or the "View Schedule" card on your dashboard.

### Navigate the Schedule
- Use the **◀ ▶ arrows** to change weeks
- Click the **center date** to return to the current week
- Switch between **list view** (recommended on mobile) and **week view** using the buttons at the top right

### Understanding Class Cards
Each class shows:
- **Time** and duration
- **Class name** (with a colored dot by type)
- **Coach** (with photo if available)
- **Remaining spots** (e.g., 3/8)

### Book a Class
1. Find the desired class in the schedule
2. Click the **Book** button
3. Booking is instant — a credit of the matching type is automatically consumed
4. The button changes to ✅ **Booked**

> **No credits?** A message will tell you that you don't have available credits for this class type. Go to **Buy a Pack** to get some.

### Full Class — Waitlist
If a class shows **Full**:
1. Click **Waitlist**
2. Your position in the queue is displayed (e.g., "Waitlisted (2)")
3. If a spot opens up, you'll receive a **notification**: "Spot available!"
4. You then have **2 hours** to confirm by clicking the orange **Confirm my spot** button
5. After that deadline, the spot is offered to the next person

To leave the waitlist, click the **✕** next to the badge.

### Special Events
Some classes have a **custom title** (e.g., "Sports Nutrition Conference"). They appear with their title in bold and the class type below.

## My Bookings

This page lists all your bookings, split into two tabs:

### Upcoming
- Your next sessions with date, time, class name, and coach
- **Cancel** button to cancel a booking (credits are not automatically refunded — contact administration if needed)

### Past
- History of past and cancelled sessions
- Each booking shows its status: **Confirmed** or **Cancelled**

---

## My Packs and Credits

### View Your Packs
The **My Packs** page shows all your packs:
- **Pack name** and credit type
- **Progress bar**: remaining credits / total credits
- **Expiry date** (in red if < 14 days)
- **Price paid**
- Expired or used-up packs appear grayed out

### Buy a Pack
1. Go to **Buy a Pack**
2. Browse packs available for your member category
3. Each card shows:
   - Credit type
   - Number of credits included
   - Validity period
   - Total price and price per session
4. Click **Buy**
5. You'll be redirected to the secure **Stripe** payment page
6. After payment, your credits are immediately activated and you receive a notification

> The most popular pack is highlighted with a **Popular** badge.

## Profile

### Edit Your Profile
1. Click your **avatar** in the header → **Profile**
2. You can edit:
   - Display name
   - First and last name
   - Phone number
   - Member category
   - Bio
3. Click **Save**

### Public Profile
Other members can view your public profile (display name and bio only).

---

## Notifications

- The **bell** 🔔 in the header shows the number of unread notifications
- Click it to access the notifications page
- Notification types:
  - ✅ Pack assigned or purchased
  - 📅 Spot available (waitlist)
  - ℹ️ Studio information
- **Mark all as read** button to clear notifications

## Language and Theme

### Change Language
Click **FR** or **EN** in the header. The language is automatically detected on first visit based on your browser.

### Change Theme
Click the ☀️/🌙 icon in the header to choose from 4 themes:
- **Classic** — Light background, clean
- **Dark** — Dark background, easy on the eyes
- **Vivid** — Light background with vibrant colors
- **Vivid Dark** — Dark background with vibrant colors

Your choice is saved automatically.

## Install the App (PWA)

Back on Track is a progressive web app. You can install it on your device to access it like a native app:

- **iPhone / iPad**: open Safari → Share button → **Add to Home Screen**
- **Android**: open Chrome → Menu ⋮ → **Install app**
- **Desktop**: Chrome → install icon in the address bar

Once installed, the app is accessible from your home screen and works even with limited connectivity.
